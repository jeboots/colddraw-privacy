Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 15, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Cold Draw ("the App") handles your information. By using Cold Draw, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Information We Do Not Collect

Cold Draw does not collect any of the following:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, address)
  • Account credentials — no account or login is required
  • Journal entries, ratings, tasting notes, or session logs
  • Location data
  • Contacts or camera data

All journal data is stored locally on your device using Android's built-in storage. This data is never transmitted to any server and is not accessible to the developer.

Advertising

Cold Draw is free and supported by advertising. We use Google AdMob to serve ads within the app. Google AdMob may collect and use certain information to provide personalized ads, including:

  • Device identifiers (Advertising ID)
  • General location (country/region level)
  • App usage data for ad relevance
  • Device type and operating system

This data is collected by Google, not by Cold Draw. Cold Draw never accesses or stores this data. For more information on how Google uses data, please visit Google's Privacy Policy.

You can opt out of personalized advertising at any time through your device settings under Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization.

Data Storage

All journal entries, ratings, and session data are stored exclusively on your device using Android AsyncStorage. This data is:

  • Never uploaded to any server
  • Never shared with any third party
  • Only accessible within the Cold Draw app on your device
  • Deleted permanently when you uninstall the app

Age Restriction

Cold Draw is intended for users aged 21 and older. Tobacco products are intended for legal-age consumers only. By using this app you confirm that you are of legal age to purchase and use tobacco products in your jurisdiction.

Children's Privacy

Cold Draw is not directed at children under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect any information from children. If you believe a child has used this app, please contact us and we will take appropriate steps.
